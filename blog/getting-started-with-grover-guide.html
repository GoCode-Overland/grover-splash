What is Grover?

Grover is your vanlife community app designed to help you reach joyful outcomes faster, whether you're planning your next adventure, troubleshooting a van issue, or discovering incredible new spots. Think of us as your co-pilot for the road ahead, built in collaboration with real van builders and travelers to make sure every interaction actually helps.

We're not just another app. We're a community-powered tool designed to make vanlife feel lighter, smoother, and way more fun. How to Download Grover

Getting Grover on your device is quick and easy. We've made sure the Grover app onboarding process is as smooth as possible, whether you prefer using your phone or browsing on the web.

iPhone App Download

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone
  2. Search for "Grover - Van Life"
  3. Look for our distinctive van icon
  4. Tap "Get" to download and install
  5. Once installed, tap the Grover icon to open

Web Access

Prefer using Grover on your laptop or desktop? No problem! You can access Grover through your web browser if your manufacturer has partnered with Grover:

  1. Visit your manufacturers platform (your development partner will provide the specific URL)
  2. No download required ‚Äì just open and start chatting
  3. Works on any modern browser

Troubleshooting Download Issues

If you're having trouble with the download process, here are a few quick fixes:

  • Check that you're connected to WiFi or have a strong cellular signal

Picking Your Rig & Agent

One of the coolest parts of getting started with Grover is choosing your setup. This helps us tailor your experience to match your specific vanlife style and needs.

Selecting Your Rig Type

During setup, you'll choose from different van categories:

  • Sam the Sage: Goes through the specs of your van one by one so we can build you a custom agent
  • Grover General Travel Planning Only: Only for planning awesome adventures
  • Sprinter Van: Mercedes Sprinter-based builds
  • Transit Van: Ford Transit conversions
  • Promaster Van: Ram Promaster-based builds or conversions

Don't worry if you're not sure or if you have multiple rigs ‚Äì you can always update this later in your profile settings. However, if you do want an assistant just for your rig we can build you one, just interact with Sam the Sage and we can use those specs and map it to your custom assistant!

Choosing Your Agent

Your Grover agent is like having a specialized vanlife expert in your pocket. Different agents have different knowledge bases and specialties:

  • Custom agent: Great for trip planning and specific questions about your systems
  • Travel Planning Agents: Great for trip planning, maybe you have mastered the systems in your van and don't need help
  • Brand Specific Agent: Agent for rigs that are produced by manufacturers like AVC Rig, these agents are preset with the components that are common in those van types, ask your manufacturer if they are a partner with Grover

If you can't see your preferred agent options, reach out to us and we'll help you get connected to the right expertise for your needs.
