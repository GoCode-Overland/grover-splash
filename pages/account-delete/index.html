Delete Account or Data

Use this form to request deletion of your Grover account and/or personal data. Requests are reviewed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your request is processed.

How to submit a deletion request:
  1. Enter the email address linked to your Grover account.
  2. Choose whether to delete your full account or specific data only.
  3. Select the data types you want deleted.
  4. Check the confirmation box and submit.
What is deleted and what is kept:
Upon a verified request, GoCode LLC will delete the selected data types from active systems within 30 days. Anonymized aggregate analytics that cannot be linked back to you may be retained. Backup copies are purged within 90 days. If required by law (e.g. tax records), certain transactional records may be retained for up to 7 years.
