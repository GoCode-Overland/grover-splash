📧 Email Campaign

Purpose: Three-email nurture series

▼

Email 1: The Announcement

Subject Line

Introducing [Brand Name]'s Circle on Grover 🗺️

Email Body

Hey [Brand Name] family, We built something for you. It's called [Brand Name]'s Circle on Grover—your new home base for adventures, answers, and everything in between. Here's what's inside: • Your Trophy Map – Share your favorite spots with fellow owners (or keep them secret). Every pin tells a story. • Your AI Assistant – Get instant answers about your specific [Brand Name] van. Heater acting up at 11 PM? We've got you. Need to know your off-grid capacity before a big trip? Just ask. • Your Community – Connect with owners who get it. The ones hitting the same trails, asking the same questions, and living the same dream. This isn't just another app. It's built specifically for your [Brand Name] rig, trained on your manuals, and packed with the knowledge we've earned building these vans. Join [Brand Name]'s Circle: [CTA Link] Let's get out there, [Brand Signature]

Email 2: The Deep Dive

Subject Line

Your van just got smarter

Email Body

Quick question: What do you do when something goes sideways on a Friday night in the middle of nowhere? You chat with your van. [Brand Name]'s Circle on Grover gives you a direct line to answers—24/7, whether we're in the shop or not. Ask anything about your rig and get responses tailored to your specific build. It's like having us in your passenger seat. Real scenarios we've solved: • "How do I winterize my water system?" ✓ • "What's error code E3 on my heater?" ✓ • "How many days can I run my fridge off-grid?" ✓ • "How do I adjust my suspension for heavy loads?" ✓ And when you do need human support? Grover creates the ticket for you so we're already in the loop. But here's the best part: while you're getting answers, you're also building your Trophy Map. Drop pins at your favorite campsites, trailheads, and hidden gems. Share them with the Circle or keep them to yourself. Either way, you're creating a legacy of adventures. Join the Circle and ask your first question: [CTA Link] See you out there, [Brand Signature]

Email 3: The Community Call

Subject Line

The best spots are the ones you share

Email Body

You know that feeling when you nail a campsite? Perfect view, no crowds, just right? Now imagine having a whole map of those spots—vetted by people who drive the same rig as you. That's the Trophy Map inside [Brand Name]'s Circle on Grover. Our owners are out there every weekend, finding the gems and sharing them with the community. Climbers, bikers, skiers, adventurers—they're all adding pins and building something incredible together. This is your network. Your spots. Your people. Plus, you've got your AI assistant ready whenever you need it. Quick question before you hit the road? Just ask. Something weird happening with your systems? We'll walk you through it. We built this for one reason: to help you spend more time adventuring and less time troubleshooting or searching for answers. Join [Brand Name]'s Circle: [CTA Link] Adventure better, [Brand Signature]
