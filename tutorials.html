joining your circle

In this video, I guide you through the process of joining your community on Grover, which is currently invite-only.

  1. Request an invite using the link from your Circle's owner (your van builder or the brand's manager)
  2. Download the app and sign up using your email address
  3. Fill out your profile and select your rig

Now you're ready to access your Trophy Map and start chatting with your van! Don't let anything stand between you and your next adventure.

creating and sharing pins

In this video, I introduce the pin creation and sharing features on Grover. You can create a shared map that only your Circle can see. To add a pin, simply upload a photo, include important details, and choose whether to share it or keep it private.

I encourage you to start exploring and building your shared map today!
